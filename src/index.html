About Me

"Singer/Codewriter"

I moved to Nashville in 2006 to pursue a life as a songwriter and recording artist. I love writing and performing, but somewhere amidst the endless booking emails and hours of solo driving I began to explore other creative outlets, and the one that I've fallen in love with is software development. After doing my own independent study for a couple of years, I decided to turbo boost my education by attending Nashville Software School. It's been a powerful experience discovering the front-end capabilities of Javascript and React, and soon what can be done on the back-end with Python/Django.
