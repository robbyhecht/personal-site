About Me

"Singer-Codewriter"

I moved to Nashville to pursue a life as a songwriter and recording artist, and since then I've released five albums and toured throughout most of the Unites States and overseas. I love writing and performing, but somewhere amidst the endless booking emails and hours of solo driving I began to explore other creative outlets, and through that process came my passion for software development. After doing my own independent study for a couple of years, I decided to intensify my education by attending Nashville Software School. It was a powerful experience learning full-stack development through building an array of applications in both group sprints and solo projects. With my background in English, education and performance, I offer a unique and creative skillset that goes beyond memorization and repetition. I'm excited to apply what I've learned and continue growing in the workplace.
